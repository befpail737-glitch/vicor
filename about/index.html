About LiTong Group

An authorized Vicor distributor with over 15 years of expertise in power electronics solutions, providing genuine products, technical support, and global sales network coverage.

Powering Innovation Through Technical Excellence

Founded in 2008, LiTong Group has established itself as a leading authorized distributor for Vicor, specializing in high-density power modules, DC-DC converters, and advanced power management solutions. Our commitment to technical excellence and customer service has made us a trusted partner for companies worldwide seeking reliable power electronics solutions.

As an authorized Vicor distributor, we provide genuine products with full warranty and support, ensuring our customers receive the highest quality and performance from their power systems. Our team combines deep technical expertise with comprehensive market knowledge to deliver superior value and service.

Our Power Electronics Expertise

Deep Understanding of Power Design Challenges

Our team has extensive experience with the complex challenges of modern power design, including high-density applications, thermal management, EMI/EMC considerations, and dynamic load requirements. We understand the critical importance of power integrity in modern electronic systems.

  • Advanced power conversion topologies (ZVS, resonant, etc.)
  • Thermal management and mechanical design
  • EMI/EMC compliance and mitigation
  • Power system architecture and optimization
  • High-performance computing power requirements

Specialization in Vicor Technologies

We have deep expertise in all Vicor product lines including Modular Power (DCM, BCM, PRM, VTM), Input Filter modules, and Picor solutions. Our team is specially trained on Vicor's advanced packaging technologies including ChiP, VIA, and other high-density approaches.
