The DCM High Power Series features ultra-high-efficiency, fixed-ratio bus converters with galvanic isolation designed for high-power applications. These modules utilize Vicor's ZVS technology to achieve up to 96.1% efficiency at power levels up to 1.6kW in VIA packaging, making them ideal for demanding applications requiring efficient high-power DC-DC conversion without regulation.

Overview

The DCM High Power Series represents Vicor's solution for high-power applications requiring efficient, isolated DC-DC conversion without regulation. These devices are specifically optimized for high-power systems where efficiency and power density are critical.

The fixed-ratio transformation (typically 4:1 or 2:1) makes them ideal for high-power bus conversion applications in data centers, telecommunications, and industrial systems. The isolated design provides safety isolation and noise immunity while enabling bidirectional power flow.

Key Features

Ultra-High Efficiency

Up to 96.1% efficiency achieved through advanced ZVS technology reduces power losses and thermal management requirements in high-power systems.

High Power Density

Delivers up to 1.6kW power in VIA package, providing superior power density for space-constrained applications.

Galvanic Isolation

2250Vdc isolation barrier provides safety isolation and prevents ground loops in distributed high-power systems.

Fixed Ratio Conversion

Achieves 4:1, 2:1, or other fixed ratios depending on model, with tight voltage tracking between input and output even at high power levels.

Design Considerations

Thermal Management

Though DCM High Power modules have exceptional efficiency, thermal management remains critical at full power. Use appropriate heat sinking and consider the thermal resistance from junction to case. Pay attention to input and output capacitor placement to minimize conducted noise and ensure stable operation at high power levels.

Layout Recommendations

For optimal performance in high-power applications, ensure adequate PCB copper for heat spreading and proper thermal via placement for vertical heat transfer. Minimize current loops to reduce EMI at high power levels. DCMs benefit from the low switching losses due to ZVS operation, which reduces thermal management challenges.

System Integration

DCM High Power modules excel in applications where you need a simple, efficient DC-DC conversion without regulation at high power levels. They are ideal for bus conversion applications where downstream converters handle regulation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do DCM High Power modules differ from standard DCM modules?

DCM High Power modules are specifically designed for higher power applications, offering up to 1.6kW in VIA packaging with enhanced thermal design. Standard DCM modules cover a broader range of power levels.

What are the thermal management considerations for DCM High Power modules?

Although highly efficient, DCM High Power modules still dissipate significant heat at full power. Proper thermal management through heatsinking and airflow is critical for reliable operation.

Can DCM High Power modules be paralleled for higher power?

Yes, DCM High Power modules can be paralleled for higher power applications, but ensure proper current sharing through careful design of the power distribution network.
