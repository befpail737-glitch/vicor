The BCM Aerospace Series features high-reliability current multiplication bus converters with bidirectional power flow designed specifically for aerospace, defense, and space applications. These modules are engineered for extreme environmental conditions and include options for radiation tolerance, ensuring consistent high-current performance in harsh operational environments.

Overview

The BCM Aerospace Series represents Vicor's solution for aerospace and defense applications requiring current multiplication with preserved dynamics. These devices are specifically designed and tested to meet stringent aerospace industry requirements for environmental conditions, reliability, and performance in extreme temperatures, vibration, and radiation environments.

The fixed-ratio transformation (typically 1:4, 1:2, or 1:1) makes them ideal for applications where you need high current at a lower voltage from a higher voltage source in aerospace systems. The bidirectional design enables power flow in both directions, suitable for satellite power systems and other aerospace applications.

Key Features

Current Multiplication

BCM modules multiply current while reducing voltage based on the turns ratio, ideal for high-current aerospace applications.

Enhanced Reliability

Engineered for aerospace and defense applications with extensive life testing and enhanced screening for high-reliability performance.

Radiation Hardened Options

Available with radiation hardened options for space exploration and satellite applications with total ionizing dose (TID) and single event effects (SEE) protection.

Hermetic Packaging

Available in hermetically sealed packages for applications requiring maximum protection from environmental contaminants.

Design Considerations

Extreme Environment Operation

BCM Aerospace modules are designed for extreme temperature ranges (-55Â°C to +125Â°C) and tested for vibration, shock, and radiation exposure. Design must account for derating in extreme conditions to maintain reliability and performance.

Radiation Effects

For space applications, consider total ionizing dose (TID) and single event effects (SEE) impacts. Radiation hardened options are available to ensure continued operation in high-radiation environments.

System Integration

BCM Aerospace modules excel in applications where you need high current multiplication with preserved dynamics in extreme environments. They are ideal for applications where load transients are handled by the upstream power source.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between standard and aerospace-qualified BCM modules?

Aerospace-qualified BCM modules undergo additional testing and screening for extreme temperature, vibration, shock, and radiation tolerance. They may also include hermetic packaging options.

What radiation tolerance options are available?

BCM Aerospace modules are available with radiation hardened options providing protection against total ionizing dose (TID) and single event effects (SEE) for space applications.

What certifications do these modules meet?

BCM Aerospace modules are designed to meet aerospace industry standards for environmental testing, reliability, and performance in accordance with various space and defense requirements.
