Picor Power Shelf provides integrated digital power management systems featuring multiple power rails and PMBus interface. These complete power solutions offer intelligent control and monitoring for complex power architectures in demanding applications.

Overview

Picor Power Shelf represents Vicor's solution for complex power management, offering multiple power rails with centralized digital control.

These systems provide complete power solutions with intelligent management, monitoring, and adaptive control for applications requiring multiple voltage rails.

Key Features

Multiple Power Rails

Integrated solution providing multiple output voltage rails in a single system with centralized control.

PMBus Interface

Comprehensive digital communication interface for configuration, monitoring, and control of all power rails.

Intelligent Management

Advanced algorithms coordinate power delivery across multiple rails for optimized performance.

System Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of voltage, current, temperature, and other critical parameters across all rails.

Design Considerations

System Integration

Proper integration requires careful planning of power rail assignments and load balancing. Consider the interaction between different rails and the central management system.

Configuration and Programming

The power shelf requires configuration for the specific application requirements. Vicor provides development tools and software to facilitate system configuration and tuning.

Thermal Management

Power shelf systems can generate significant heat at full loading. Ensure adequate cooling and thermal management in the system design for reliable operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMBus?

PMBus (Power Management Bus) is a standardized digital communication protocol specifically designed for power management applications. It allows bidirectional communication between power controllers and host systems for monitoring and control.

Can Power Shelf systems be expanded?

Yes, Power Shelf systems can be configured with different numbers of rails and power levels to meet specific application requirements.

What monitoring capabilities are provided?

Power Shelf systems provide real-time monitoring of voltage, current, temperature, and other parameters across all power rails with centralized control and status reporting.
