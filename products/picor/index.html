As an authorized distributor, we provide Picor Solutions including Cool-Power ZVS DC-DC converters. These high-efficiency, low-noise power solutions offer innovative approaches to power conversion challenges across diverse applications.

Picor Solutions Overview

Picor Solutions, now part of the Vicor family, offer specialized power conversion solutions that complement Vicor's modular power portfolio. The Cool-Power series features ZVS (Zero Voltage Switching) technology that delivers high efficiency and low EMI in compact packages. Picor's expertise in digital power control and advanced topology design provides unique solutions for challenging power applications.

These solutions are designed for applications requiring high efficiency, low noise, excellent thermal performance, and compact form factors. Picor's ZVS technology significantly reduces switching losses and electromagnetic interference, making these converters ideal for sensitive applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does ZVS technology improve system efficiency?

ZVS technology eliminates switching losses during turn-on, significantly reducing power losses, especially at higher switching frequencies.

What applications benefit most from Cool-Power converters?

Any application requiring high efficiency, low EMI, and reliable power conversion benefits from Cool-Power technology.

Can Cool-Power converters be used in parallel?

Some Cool-Power models include current sharing capability for parallel operation to increase power output.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMBus and how does it benefit my design?

PMBus is a standardized digital power management protocol that enables communication with and control of power converters.

How does digital control improve converter performance?

Digital control enables adaptive algorithms that optimize performance based on real-time operating conditions.

Is there a performance trade-off with digital solutions?

Modern digital controllers achieve performance equal to or better than analog solutions with added flexibility.
