Picor Solutions by Vicor

Power Management Innovation by Picor

Picor Solutions brings specialized power management expertise to the Vicor family of power solutions. The Picor portfolio includes Cool-Power ZVS devices that provide point-of-regulation with exceptional efficiency and low electromagnetic interference (EMI).

Picor's zero-voltage switching (ZVS) technology addresses the growing need for high-efficiency power conversion in space-constrained applications while maintaining excellent EMI performance. These solutions complement Vicor's modular power portfolio and provide design flexibility for various power system architectures.

Technology Advantages

Zero Voltage Switching (ZVS) Technology

ZVS technology eliminates switching losses by turning transistors on and off when the voltage across them is zero. This results in:

  • Higher efficiency, especially at higher frequencies
  • Reduced electromagnetic interference (EMI)
  • Lower thermal stress on components
  • Ability to operate at higher switching frequencies
  • Improved reliability due to reduced stress
