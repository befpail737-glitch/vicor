Design Considerations

System Integration

Proper integration requires careful design of the communication bus and appropriate pull-up resistors. Consider noise immunity when routing the PMBus interface in electrically noisy environments.

Configuration and Programming

The power supplies typically require configuration for the specific application requirements. Vicor provides development tools and software to facilitate system configuration and tuning.

Thermal Management

While the digital control provides efficiency optimization, thermal management remains important in enclosed systems. Ensure adequate cooling for reliable operation at full power.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMBus?

PMBus (Power Management Bus) is a standardized digital communication protocol specifically designed for power management applications. It allows bidirectional communication between power controllers and host systems for monitoring and control.

Can Picor Power Supplies work with non-Vicor components?

Yes, Picor Power Supplies are designed to work in systems with various components from different manufacturers.

What protection features are included?

Picor Power Supplies include overvoltage, overcurrent, and overtemperature protection with digital monitoring and status reporting capabilities.
