Automotive Power Solutions

Vicor's high-density, high-efficiency power solutions for automotive applications including electric vehicles, advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), infotainment, and automotive control systems. Our solutions meet the demanding requirements for automotive environments while maximizing electric vehicle range.

Powering the Future of Transportation

Automotive applications demand power systems with exceptional reliability, efficiency, and resilience to harsh environments. With the increasing electrification of vehicles, power density and efficiency have become critical factors in enabling electric vehicle adoption. Vicor's power solutions are engineered to meet these challenges, providing high power density while maintaining the reliability required for automotive safety systems.

Our solutions address the unique challenges of automotive environments: wide temperature ranges, electrical noise, vibration, and stringent safety requirements. The high efficiency of our power modules is particularly critical in electric vehicles, where every watt contributes to extended driving range.

Key Application Areas

Electric Vehicle Powertrain

High-efficiency power conversion for electric vehicle systems including battery management, motor control, and charging systems. Our solutions maximize range by minimizing power losses while providing the reliability required for automotive applications.

  • DC-DC converters

Reliable power for safety-critical ADAS systems including radar, lidar, cameras, and autonomous driving computers. Our isolated power solutions provide galvanic isolation between safety-critical systems for enhanced safety.

High-current power for automotive processors, infotainment systems, and digital cockpits. Our current multiplication solutions provide the high current density required for modern automotive processors.

Power solutions for EV charging stations from Level 1 to fast DC charging systems. Our high-density solutions enable compact charger designs while maintaining high efficiency.

Addressing Automotive Power Challenges

Efficiency for Extended Range

In electric vehicles, power conversion efficiency directly impacts driving range. Vicor's ZVS technology provides exceptional efficiency across wide load and temperature ranges, maximizing range by minimizing power losses.

Our high-efficiency modules can achieve up to 96%+ efficiency in automotive applications, which is critical in battery-constrained systems.

Harsh Environmental Conditions

Automotive systems must operate in extreme temperatures, vibration, and electrical noise. Vicor's power modules are designed with automotive-grade components and robust construction to ensure reliable operation.

Our solutions are tested across the full automotive temperature range (-40Â°C to +125Â°C) with derating to ensure reliable operation.

EMI/EMC Compliance

Automotive systems must comply with strict electromagnetic compatibility standards to avoid interference with radio, navigation, and safety systems. Vicor's power modules have excellent EMI characteristics and can be combined with our filtering solutions for standards compliance.

Our solutions meet automotive EMI/EMC standards including CISPR 25 and similar regulations for conducted and radiated emissions.

Safety and Reliability

Automotive safety-critical systems require exceptional reliability and fail-safe operation. Vicor's power modules use high-quality components and conservative design practices to meet automotive reliability requirements.

Our solutions support functional safety requirements (ASIL levels) and can be designed into redundant systems for enhanced safety.

Automotive Standards Compliance

Meeting Automotive Requirements

Automotive power systems must comply with stringent standards for safety, electromagnetic compatibility, and environmental performance. Vicor's power solutions are designed to meet these requirements:

  • AEC-Q100 (Stress test qualification for ICs)
  • ISO 26262 (Road vehicles functional safety)
  • CISPR 25 (EMI emissions for vehicles)
  • IEC 61000 (EMC compatibility requirements)

Our applications engineering team has extensive experience with automotive standards and can assist with design verification and compliance validation.

Automotive Power Design Considerations

Thermal Management

Automotive environments often have limited cooling options and high ambient temperatures. Vicor's high-efficiency modules generate less heat, reducing thermal management challenges. Our thermal designs are optimized for automotive cooling approaches including conduction and convection.

Modular Power Architecture

Automotive systems benefit from distributed power architectures where power modules are placed close to load points. This approach reduces IÂ²R losses in distribution and provides cleaner power to sensitive systems like ADAS sensors. Vicor's small form factor modules are ideal for distributed architectures.

Redundancy and Safety

Safety-critical automotive systems often require redundant power designs. Vicor's modular approach enables redundant power system designs where multiple converters can share load or provide backup operation for enhanced safety.
