Aerospace Power Solutions

Vicor's high-density, high-efficiency power solutions for aerospace applications including avionics, satellite systems, flight control, and spacecraft power systems. Our solutions meet the stringent requirements for weight, efficiency, and reliability in aerospace environments.

Powering Critical Aerospace Systems

Aerospace applications demand the most stringent requirements for power systems: maximum efficiency, minimal weight, extreme reliability, and operation in harsh environments. Vicor's power solutions are specifically engineered to meet these challenges, providing high power density while maintaining the reliability required for safety-critical aerospace applications.

Our solutions address the unique challenges of aerospace environments: wide temperature ranges, vibration, radiation, and the critical need for weight reduction. The high efficiency of our power modules reduces thermal loads, which is critical in the difficult thermal environment of aircraft and spacecraft.

Addressing Aerospace Power Challenges

Weight and Size Constraints

In aerospace applications, every gram matters. Vicor's high power density solutions significantly reduce the weight and size of power systems compared to traditional approaches, contributing to overall vehicle performance and fuel efficiency.

Our ChiP (Converter housed in Package) and VIA (Vertical Integration Architecture) packaging technologies provide exceptional power density without compromising performance or reliability.

Thermal Management in Extreme Environments

Aerospace applications often require operation in extreme temperatures and with limited cooling options. Vicor's high-efficiency power modules generate less heat, reducing thermal management challenges.

Our solutions also offer excellent thermal performance for the installed package, with options for enhanced heat transfer to accommodate various cooling approaches.

Vibration and Shock Resistance

Aerospace systems must withstand extreme mechanical stress during operation. Vicor's power modules are designed for high reliability in vibration and shock environments.

Our devices use minimal external components and robust packaging to ensure reliable operation in challenging mechanical environments.

Radiation Tolerance for Space Applications

For space applications, Vicor offers radiation-tolerant power solutions designed to operate reliably in the harsh radiation environment of space.

Our space-qualified devices meet the stringent requirements for total ionizing dose (TID) and single event effects (SEE) for long-term operation in space environments.

Aerospace Power Design Considerations

Redundancy and Safety

Aerospace power systems often require redundant designs to ensure continued operation in case of component failure. Vicor's modular approach facilitates redundant power system designs where multiple converters can share load or provide backup operation.

EMI/EMC Compliance

Aerospace systems must meet strict electromagnetic compatibility requirements. Vicor's power modules, combined with our input filtering solutions, help achieve compliance with aerospace EMI/EMC standards.

Derating for Reliability

Power components in aerospace applications are typically operated well below their maximum ratings to enhance reliability and safety margins. Vicor's high-efficiency modules maintain excellent performance even at low load conditions, making them ideal for derated aerospace designs.
