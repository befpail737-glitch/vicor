High-reliability power solutions engineered for the extreme requirements of aerospace and defense applications. Our power modules meet rigorous specifications for space, aviation, and military applications where performance and reliability are critical.

Power Solutions for Extreme Environments

Aerospace and defense applications demand power solutions that perform flawlessly in the most challenging conditions: extreme temperatures, high vibration, radiation exposure, and vacuum environments. Our power modules are designed and tested to meet these rigorous requirements while delivering the high efficiency and power density essential for weight and space-constrained systems.

From commercial aviation to space exploration, from ground-based radar systems to naval vessels, our solutions provide the reliability and performance that mission-critical applications demand.

Aerospace Design Considerations

Thermal Management

Special considerations for aerospace environments:

  • In space applications, rely on radiation for heat dissipation
  • For aviation, consider convection and conduction cooling
  • High-altitude operation reduces convective cooling efficiency
  • Thermal cycling in orbit applications
  • Thermal shock during launch phases

Reliability & Redundancy

Strategies for ensuring high-reliability operation:

  • Detailed reliability predictions (MTBF)
  • Derating components for longer life
  • Redundant power system design
  • Self-diagnostics and health monitoring
  • Fail-safe operational modes

Radiation Effects

Understanding and mitigating radiation exposure:

  • Total Ionizing Dose (TID) effects
  • Single Event Effects (SEE) considerations
  • Selection of radiation-tolerant components
  • Shielding strategies
  • Operational margin planning

Launch Environment

Surviving the extreme launch environment:

  • High acceleration forces during launch
  • Acoustic vibration during liftoff
  • Pyro-shock from stage separation
  • Vibration across wide frequency range
  • Ensuring no mechanical resonance
