Vicor's high-density, high-efficiency power solutions for robotics applications including mobile robots, industrial automation, autonomous systems, and service robots. Our solutions address the unique challenges of robotic power systems: mobility constraints, dynamic loads, and space limitations.

Powering the Future of Robotics

Robotics applications present unique power challenges: mobile operation with limited power sources, dynamic loads from motors and actuators, space constraints in compact designs, and the need for high efficiency to maximize operational time. Vicor's power solutions are engineered to meet these challenges with high power density and exceptional efficiency.

Our solutions provide stable, clean power for the diverse systems within robotics applications: computing and AI, sensing, motor control, and communication systems. The high efficiency of our power modules extends battery life in mobile robots while reducing thermal management needs.

Addressing Robotics Power Challenges

Battery Life Optimization

In mobile robotics, battery life directly affects operational time. Vicor's high-efficiency power modules minimize power losses, extending operational time between charges. Our ZVS technology maintains high efficiency even at light loads typical in robotics.

The high efficiency allows for smaller battery systems without sacrificing performance, reducing overall weight and improving robot mobility.

Dynamic Load Handling

Robotics applications present highly dynamic loads from motors and actuators. Vicor's power modules offer excellent transient response to handle these rapidly changing load conditions without compromising performance.

The fast transient response prevents brownouts during high-current events, ensuring stable operation of sensitive systems like sensors and processors.

Space and Weight Constraints

Robotics designs often have severe space constraints. Vicor's high power density solutions minimize the space required for power systems, allowing more space for functionality and payload.

Our ChiP and VIA packaging technologies provide exceptional power density, enabling more compact robot designs without sacrificing power capability.

Motor Drive Power Systems

Robotic systems require high-current power for motor drivers and actuators. Vicor's current multiplication modules (BCM, VTM) are optimized for these applications, providing high current from distributed power rails.

Our power modules can handle the high peak currents required for robotic motion while maintaining system efficiency and stability.

Robotics Power Design Considerations

Modular Power Architecture

Robotics applications benefit from distributed power architectures where power modules are placed close to load points. Redundancy and Safety

Many robotics applications, particularly in autonomous systems, require redundant power systems for fail-safe operation. Vicor's modular approach enables redundant power system designs where multiple converters can share load or provide backup operation.

Thermal Management

Robotics applications often have limited cooling options. Vicor's high-efficiency modules generate less heat, reducing thermal management challenges. The thermal design of our packages is optimized for efficient heat transfer in space-constrained applications.
