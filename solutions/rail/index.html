Rail Power Solutions

Vicor's high-density, high-efficiency power solutions for railway applications including locomotives, transit vehicles, rail infrastructure, and signaling systems. Our solutions meet the demanding requirements for reliability, efficiency, and environmental resilience in rail environments.

Powering Reliable Rail Operations

Railway applications demand power systems with exceptional reliability, long operational life, and resilience to harsh environmental conditions. Vicor's power solutions are engineered to meet these challenges, providing high power density while maintaining the reliability and durability required for safety-critical rail applications.

Our solutions address the unique challenges of rail environments: wide temperature ranges, shock and vibration, electromagnetic interference, and long-term reliability requirements. The high efficiency of our power modules reduces thermal loads and maintenance requirements.

Key Application Areas

Locomotive Power Systems

Reliable power for locomotive control systems, traction motor control, and auxiliary systems. Our solutions provide the stable, clean power required for critical locomotive functions while withstanding harsh operating environments.

  • Traction control systems
  • Locomotive electronics
  • Heating and cooling systems
  • Communication systems

Transit Vehicle Power

High-reliability power for passenger rail systems including subways, light rail, and commuter trains. Our solutions ensure consistent performance for passenger comfort and safety systems.

  • Passenger comfort systems
  • Door control systems
  • Emergency lighting
  • Entertainment systems

Rail Infrastructure

Power for wayside equipment including signaling, communication, and control systems. Our solutions provide reliable power in challenging environmental conditions with minimal maintenance requirements.

  • Signaling systems
  • Trackside electronics
  • Communication equipment
  • Control rooms

Signaling and Safety Systems

Safety-critical systems requiring the highest levels of reliability and fail-safe operation. Our isolated power solutions provide galvanic isolation between safety-critical systems for enhanced safety.

  • Positive train control
  • Automatic train protection
  • Track circuitry
  • Warning systems

Addressing Rail Power Challenges

Shock and Vibration Resistance

Rail systems experience significant mechanical stress from track irregularities and long-term operation. Vicor's power modules are designed for high reliability in vibration and shock environments with robust mechanical construction.

Our devices use minimal external components and compact packaging to ensure reliable operation in challenging mechanical environments.

Wide Temperature Operation

Rail systems must operate in extreme temperatures from arctic conditions to desert environments. Vicor's power modules are specified and tested across wide temperature ranges to ensure reliable operation.

Our solutions maintain performance and reliability across the full temperature range with minimal parameter drift.

Electromagnetic Compatibility

Rail environments have significant electromagnetic interference from traction motors, communication systems, and power lines. Vicor's power modules are designed with excellent EMI characteristics and can be combined with our filtering solutions for compliance.

Our solutions meet the stringent EMI/EMC requirements for rail applications, ensuring reliable operation in electrically noisy environments.

Long-Term Reliability

Rail systems have 30+ year operational requirements with minimal maintenance. Vicor's power modules use high-quality components and conservative design practices to achieve exceptional long-term reliability.

Our solutions undergo extensive reliability testing and have demonstrated field performance in safety-critical applications.

Rail Standards Compliance

Meeting Railway Requirements

Railway power systems must comply with stringent standards for safety, electromagnetic compatibility, and environmental performance. Vicor's power solutions are designed to meet these requirements:

  • EN 50121 (Railway EMI/EMC standards)
  • EN 50155 (Railway electronic equipment standards)
  • IEC 61000 (EMC compatibility requirements)
  • IEC 60529 (Protection against ingress)

Our applications engineering team has extensive experience with rail standards and can assist with design verification and compliance validation.

Rail Power Design Considerations

Redundancy and Safety

Rail safety-critical systems often require redundant power designs to ensure continued operation in case of component failure. Vicor's modular approach facilitates redundant power system designs where multiple converters can share load or provide backup operation.

Maintenance and Serviceability

Rail systems require consideration of maintenance access and component replacement. Vicor's reliable power modules minimize maintenance requirements while our compact packaging allows for efficient use of available space for maintenance access.

Derating for Reliability

Power components in rail applications are typically operated well below their maximum ratings to enhance reliability and safety margins. Vicor's high-efficiency modules maintain excellent performance even at low load conditions, making them ideal for derated rail designs.
