New Manufacturing Facility Opens in Asia - Expanding Production Capacity

Company News • January 15, 2025 • 5 min read

Vicor Corporation has officially opened its newest advanced manufacturing facility in Asia, significantly expanding production capacity to meet the rapidly growing global demand for high-performance power modules. The state-of-the-art facility will enhance supply chain resilience and support customers worldwide with critical power solutions for advanced applications.

Investment in Advanced Manufacturing

The new facility represents a strategic investment in Vicor's ability to scale production while maintaining the exceptionally high quality standards that customers expect. Spanning 500,000 square feet, the facility incorporates Industry 4.0 technologies and automated manufacturing processes to ensure consistent quality and reliability.

"Our new manufacturing facility demonstrates our commitment to meeting customer demand for our innovative power solutions," said David Bell, CEO of Vicor Corporation. "This investment positions us to support the explosive growth in applications requiring high-efficiency, high-power-density power delivery."

Cutting-Edge Production Capabilities

The facility features several key capabilities that enhance Vicor's manufacturing excellence:

Automated Assembly Lines

Advanced robotics and automated assembly systems ensure consistent quality and efficiency in module manufacturing.

Quality Control Systems

Real-time monitoring and testing systems verify quality parameters throughout the manufacturing process.

Supply Chain Integration

Seamless integration with supplier networks ensures reliable component sourcing and just-in-time delivery.

Environmental Controls

Advanced clean room technologies and climate controls ensure optimal manufacturing conditions.

Impact on Customer Supply

The expanded capacity will directly benefit customers by:

  • Shortened Lead Times: Significantly reduced delivery times for standard products and custom solutions
  • Enhanced Availability: Improved stock levels for critical power modules
  • Scalability: Ability to support customers' growth with reliable supply
  • Quality Consistency: Uniform quality across all manufacturing locations

Technology Focus Areas

The facility will specialize in manufacturing several key product families:

ChiP Modules

High-power-density ChiP (Converter in Package) solutions for space-constrained applications.

BCM/VTM Systems

Bus Converter Modules and Vicor Transformer Modules for Factorized Power Architecture.

DCM Converters

Fixed-ratio DC-DC Converter Modules for isolated power conversion applications.

Global Manufacturing Strategy

The Asia facility complements Vicor's global manufacturing footprint, which includes facilities in the United States and Europe. This strategic geographic distribution enhances supply chain resilience and enables local support for customers worldwide.

The facility will implement Vicor's proven manufacturing methodologies and quality systems across all sites, ensuring identical product quality and performance regardless of manufacturing location. Employees will receive comprehensive training on Vicor's advanced manufacturing processes and quality standards.

Environmental and Sustainability Commitments

Environmental responsibility is central to the facility's design:

  • Renewable Energy: On-site solar installations will offset a significant portion of the facility's energy consumption
  • Water Conservation: Advanced recycling systems minimize water usage
  • Waste Reduction: Comprehensive recycling programs divert waste from landfills
  • Carbon Footprint: Energy-efficient manufacturing processes and equipment reduce overall emissions

Supporting Innovation Pipeline

The facility's scalable design supports future product introductions and technology advancements. Flexible manufacturing lines can be reconfigured to support new product families as Vicor continues to innovate in power delivery technologies.

The facility will also house R&D facilities focused on next-generation packaging technologies and power conversion methodologies. Close integration between design and manufacturing teams accelerates the transition from concept to production.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will the increased capacity be available to customers?

Production ramp-up began in January 2025, with full capacity available by mid-year. Customers can expect shortened lead times beginning in February 2025.

Will products from the new facility meet the same quality standards?

Absolutely. The new facility implements identical quality systems, processes, and testing procedures as our existing facilities. All products come with Vicor's standard warranty and performance guarantees.

Does this affect pricing for Vicor products?

At this time, there are no planned price changes as a result of this expansion. Improved manufacturing efficiency may enable competitive pricing in the future.
